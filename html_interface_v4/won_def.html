Rules of the game

The Island game is a two-player game.
The two players, Green and Orange, take turns conquering territories.
You will play Green.

The goal is to capture all three territories of one island or to obtain all three resources of one type across islands.

You can press the button at the corner of each board to view a table showing the points each player has.
Pressing will show the points Green has obtained so far,
then pressing will show the points Orange has obtained so far.
Pressing will get you back to the current board.
