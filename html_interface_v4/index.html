Do you want to become an expert of the Island game?
To do so, you will pass a trial which includes 4 parts.
You will first get familiar with the rules of the game. Then you will answer some questions about single moves.
Next you will become an apprentice of the Great Wizard. Finally, you will apply what you have learnt by answering some more questions about single moves.


First, here are some basics of the game. The Island game is a two-player game.
The two players, green and orange, take turns conquering territories on the world below.
Any untaken territory can be conquered until the game terminates.

Conquering a territory provides one or several resources. For instance, conquering the top left territory would provide two resources: one Castle and one River.
Each resource belongs to one of the following category:
Island 1, Island 2, Island 3, Forest, Castle, Cornfield, River, Animal.
The player who first succeeds in obtaining three resources of the same category wins the game.
You can press button at the corner of each board to view the table for resources acquired.

A TRIPLET is a collection of three resources of the same category.
One player has a triplet in each the following board states, the territories containing the resources of the triplet are highlighted.



A PAIR is a collection of two resources of the same category for one player, the third resource not being taken yet by the opponent.
One player has one or two pairs in each the following board states, the territories containing the resources of the pairs are highlighted.
