Definitions: Pair

Now, please read carefully the definitions below that will help you thorough the game.
Remember, you can click on the corner button of each board to view the table of resources captured.

A PAIR is a collection of two resources of the same category for one player, the third resource not being taken yet by the opponent.
One player has one or two pairs in each the following board states, the territories containing the resources of the pairs are highlighted.
